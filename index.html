Welcome to the Library

You are trapped in the library... The only way out is to fight!

Use your intellectual skills to lower the IQ of your opponent.

Each enemy will ask you a series of trivia questions. Every time you answer correctly, their IQ goes down! But be careful... Every time you answer incorrectly, your IQ goes down.

The battle ends when either fighter's IQ reaches 0.
Good luck!
